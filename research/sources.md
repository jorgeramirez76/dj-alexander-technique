## Verification log — how key facts were confirmed
- **Real identity / civilian name:** NOT published on any source found. Discogs gives only the
  stage name + birth date (13 Nov 1975). → left blank, marked `Needs verification`.
- **"Cuban-American":** stated in press bio surfaced via search and echoed on RA. Not on the
  artist's own SoundCloud bio (which says "NYC-rooted", "tri-state scene"). → included but
  flagged `Needs verification` (heritage claim).
- **Support from A-list DJs:** claimed on RA bio **and independently corroborated** by 20+ real
  YouTube clips of Carl Cox, Adam Beyer, Kevin Saunderson, Loco Dice, Maceo Plex, The Blessed
  Madonna, Marco Carola, Shiba San, Delta Funktionen dropping his tracks/remixes (source #4).
- **Labels:** Terminator Records (co-founded w/ Todd Terry) — RA + multiple Beatport releases.
  Techno Riots (launched 2024) — RA bio. Data Distortion (launched 2026) — official SoundCloud
  bio. All three are real per their respective sources; presented with dates.
- **Past shows:** taken verbatim from RA's event history (source #5). Specific years for the
  Detroit/NYC dates were not shown on the profile → flagged `Needs verification`.
