# Biography — DJ Alexander Technique

> **Verbatim official bio** (source: artist's own SoundCloud, #3). This is the authoritative
> self-description and is used as the backbone of the About page.

> "Alexander Technique is a NYC-rooted DJ, producer, and remixer known for raw, banging techno
> and a stripped, uncompromising underground sound. Emerging from the tri-state scene in the
> late '90s, he built his reputation through relentless DJ sets, deep record knowledge, and
> groove-driven productions designed for real rooms and late hours.
>
> With a career spanning more than two decades, he has collaborated with artists including
> Todd Terry, Felix Da Housecat and Roland Clark, evolving from house roots into a darker, more
> raw techno direction while maintaining true underground credibility.
>
> His productions have earned support from Carl Cox, Adam Beyer, Chris Liebing, Nicole Moudaber,
> Maceo Plex, Kevin Saunderson, Josh Wink, and The Blessed Madonna, regularly appearing in DJ
> sets across global club circuits.
>
> In 2026 he launched Data Distortion, an independent imprint dedicated to releasing his own
> raw, futuristic techno—focused on tension, groove, and a no-compromise aesthetic."
>
> Booking: bookings@djalexandertechnique.com · www.djalexandertechnique.com

## Career timeline (only sourced milestones — no invented dates)
- **Late 1990s** — Emerges from the NY/NJ tri-state underground; begins DJing house. `Source #3`
- **2007** — First event listing appears on Resident Advisor. `Source #5`
- **2010** — Official website first archived (23 Jul 2010). `Source #2`
- **2017** — "System" released on Terminator Records. `Source #11`
- **Co-founds Terminator Records** with house/techno legend Todd Terry (year `Needs verification`);
  imprint focuses on hard-groove techno. `Source #5, #6`
- **2024** — Launches **Techno Riots**. `Source #6` · "El Ritmo Santo" released on Terminator. `Source #12`
- **2026** — Launches **Data Distortion**, an independent imprint for his own raw, futuristic
  techno. Recent EP peaks at **#5 on the Beatport Techno Top 100**. `Source #3, #6`
